How It Works

Your Guide to
Buying a Property

01

Speak to Our Team

Every successful property search begins with a conversation. Tell us exactly what you are looking for — not just the number of bedrooms, but the lifestyle you are seeking. We will listen carefully and draw on our knowledge of the market to guide you in the right direction from the outset.

02

Secure Your Finances

Before beginning your search in earnest, it is important to understand your purchasing budget. If you require a mortgage, we recommend speaking with an independent mortgage adviser at the earliest opportunity. Having a mortgage agreement in principle in place will strengthen your position considerably when making an offer.

03

Viewings & Shortlisting

We arrange and accompany viewings at a time that suits you. Our team members know every property in our portfolio intimately and will be on hand throughout each viewing to answer your questions honestly and in full. We never rush — a property purchase deserves proper consideration.

04

Making an Offer

When you have found the right property, we will advise you on the appropriate level at which to pitch your offer based on current market conditions, comparable sales, and the specific circumstances of the vendor. We will present your offer professionally and negotiate on your behalf where appropriate.

05

Survey & Legal Process

Once your offer has been accepted, your solicitor will begin the legal process of conveyancing. We strongly recommend commissioning an independent survey of the property. Our team will remain actively involved throughout — chasing solicitors, managing enquiries and keeping all parties informed — until contracts are exchanged.

06

Exchange & Completion

Exchange of contracts is the legal point at which the transaction becomes binding. Completion — the day you receive the keys to your new home — typically follows within a week to a month of exchange. Our team will be with you every step of the way to ensure the process is as smooth and stress-free as possible.

Ready to begin your search? Our team would be delighted to discuss your requirements and guide you through the process from start to finish.

Where We Sell

Areas We Cover

Jesmond

Newcastle's most prestigious residential suburb, Jesmond is characterised by handsome Edwardian and Victorian properties, wide tree-lined avenues, and an exceptional quality of life. With its own Metro station, a celebrated high street, and Jesmond Dene on the doorstep, it remains consistently the most sought-after postcode in the city. Properties here range from grand five- and six-bedroom detached villas to elegant period apartments.

Average sale price: £650,000 – £1,500,000+

Gosforth

Adjoining Jesmond to the north, Gosforth is one of Newcastle's most family-friendly suburbs, known for its excellent schools, vibrant high street, and a diverse mix of property styles. Victorian and Edwardian terraces sit alongside substantial detached homes and more contemporary properties. The area offers outstanding value for families seeking size and space within easy reach of the city centre.

Average sale price: £400,000 – £1,200,000+

Ponteland

Just eight miles from Newcastle city centre, Ponteland is a desirable village that attracts buyers seeking the very best of both worlds: rural peace and village community alongside easy city access. The village itself has an excellent range of independent shops and restaurants, and is served by outstanding state and independent schools. The surrounding area offers some of the finest executive and bespoke new-build properties in the region.

Average sale price: £500,000 – £2,000,000+

Northumberland

For those seeking something truly exceptional, Northumberland offers an unrivalled portfolio of country houses, converted farmsteads, historic manors, and rural estates. With some of England's most spectacular scenery, designated Areas of Outstanding Natural Beauty, and a coastline of extraordinary beauty, this is property at its most dramatic. Our team has decades of experience in this market and maintains an extensive network of contacts across the county.

Average sale price: £500,000 – £5,000,000+
