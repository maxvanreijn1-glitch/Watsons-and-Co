Our Story

About Watsons & Co

Home / About

Founded 2004

Newcastle's Name in
Luxury Property

Watsons & Co was founded in 2004 with a clear purpose: to bring a genuinely premium estate agency experience to Newcastle upon Tyne and Northumberland. Two decades on, that purpose remains unchanged — to represent exceptional homes with the care, discretion and expertise they deserve.

From our office on Grey Street in the heart of Newcastle, we serve clients across the city's most sought-after suburbs — Jesmond, Gosforth and Ponteland — as well as the broader county of Northumberland, where spectacular country houses and rural estates remain among the finest in the north of England.

We are a deliberately boutique firm. We do not chase volume. We take on a carefully selected portfolio of the best properties in our market and dedicate the full attention of our team to each and every one. The result is a level of service — and a quality of result — that our clients have come to rely on.

— Max Watson, Founder & Director
