About Watsons & Co

Home / About

Founded 2004

Newcastle's Name in
Luxury Property

Watsons & Co was founded in 2004 with a clear purpose: to bring a genuinely premium estate agency experience to Newcastle upon Tyne and Northumberland. Two decades on, that purpose remains unchanged — to represent exceptional homes with the care, discretion and expertise they deserve.

From our office on Grey Street in the heart of Newcastle, we serve clients across the city's most sought-after suburbs — Jesmond, Gosforth and Ponteland — as well as the broader county of Northumberland, where spectacular country houses and rural estates remain among the finest in the north of England.

We are a deliberately boutique firm. We do not chase volume. We take on a carefully selected portfolio of the best properties in our market and dedicate the full attention of our team to each and every one. The result is a level of service — and a quality of result — that our clients have come to rely on.

— Max Watson, Founder & Director

Two Decades of Excellence

Our History

2004

Founded on Grey Street

Max Watson founded Watsons & Co at 14 Grey Street, Newcastle upon Tyne, with a clear vision: to create a truly premium estate agency that would handle exceptional homes with the care and discretion they deserve. From the outset, the focus was quality over quantity.

2008

Expansion into Northumberland

Following the success of our city operations, we formally extended our remit to cover the prime country house and rural estate market across Northumberland, drawing on our existing relationships with landowners, estates and rural buyers across the county.

2010

Lettings Division Established

Sarah Clarke joined as Head of Sales, and we launched our dedicated Lettings & Management division — quickly building a reputation for the most rigorous tenant selection and highest quality of ongoing property management in the North East prime market.

2016

£500 Million in Sales

A landmark moment — total cumulative sales through Watsons & Co reached £500 million, representing hundreds of families and individuals who trusted us with one of the most significant transactions of their lives. Our 98% client satisfaction rate remained unchanged throughout this growth.

2024

Two Decades and Beyond

Twenty years on from our founding, Watsons & Co continues to represent the North East's finest homes. With over £1 billion in total sales, a team of exceptional people, and an undiminished commitment to excellence, we look forward to the decades ahead with confidence and pride.
