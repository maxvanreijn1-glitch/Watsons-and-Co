Our Story

About Watsons & Co

Home / About

Founded 2026

Newcastle's New Name in
Luxury Property

Watsons & Co was founded in 2026 with a clear purpose: to bring a genuinely premium estate agency experience to Newcastle upon Tyne and Northumberland. From day one, our commitment is to represent exceptional homes with the care, discretion and expertise they deserve.

From our office on Grey Street in the heart of Newcastle, we serve clients across the city's most sought-after suburbs — Jesmond, Gosforth and Ponteland — as well as the broader county of Northumberland, where spectacular country houses and rural estates remain among the finest in the north of England.

We are a deliberately boutique firm. We do not chase volume. We take on a carefully selected portfolio of the best properties in our market and dedicate the full attention of our team to each and every one. We are building something we are proud of — and we are just getting started.

— Max Watson, Founder & Director

Why We're Here

Our Vision

2026

Founded on Grey Street

Max Watson founded Watsons & Co at 14 Grey Street, Newcastle upon Tyne, with a single clear vision: to create a truly premium estate agency that handles exceptional homes with the care and discretion they deserve. From day one, the focus is quality over quantity.

Boutique by Design

We are deliberately small. We will never take on more properties than we can represent properly. Every instruction receives the full attention of our team — not a junior negotiator — and every client will always be able to reach us directly.

Rooted in Newcastle

Newcastle upon Tyne and Northumberland are home to some of England's finest residential property. We are here because we believe this market deserves an estate agency that truly understands it — not a national chain operating from a distance.

Building Something Lasting

We are at the very beginning of our story. Our ambition is to become the name that discerning buyers and sellers across Newcastle and Northumberland turn to first — not because we are the biggest, but because we are the best. We invite you to be part of that journey from the outset.
