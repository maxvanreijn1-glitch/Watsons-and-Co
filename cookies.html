1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work, improve their efficiency, and provide information to website owners. Cookies may be "session cookies" (which expire when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (which remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

Under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and UK GDPR, we are required to inform you about the cookies we use and, where required, obtain your consent before placing them on your device.

2. Cookies We Use

These cookies are essential for the website to function and cannot be switched off. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting your privacy preferences or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block these cookies, but some parts of the site may not work as a result.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses Google Fonts and Font Awesome CDN to serve web fonts and icons. When your browser loads these resources, the providers may set cookies or collect technical data including your IP address. Please refer to Google's and Font Awesome's own privacy policies for details of how they handle this data.

We may also embed maps or other third-party content on this website. Where we do so, those third parties may set their own cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

  • Browser settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can delete existing cookies and choose whether to accept new ones. Please note that if you choose to disable cookies, some features of our website may not function correctly.
  • Opt-out tools: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on, available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Cookie preference centre: When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent notice that allows you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preferences at any time by clearing your cookies and revisiting the site.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, regulation or the cookies we use. We will notify you of any material changes by posting an updated policy on this page with a revised "last updated" date.
