Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Privacy Policy Terms & Conditions Cookie Policy Complaints AML Policy Fees & Charges Accessibility

1. Our Commitment

Watsons & Co is committed to making our website accessible to the widest possible audience, including people with disabilities. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA.

This statement applies to the website at watsonsandco.com.

2. How Accessible This Website Is

We have designed our website with accessibility in mind. The following features are currently in place:

  • Semantic HTML structure with appropriate heading hierarchy
  • All images include descriptive alternative text (alt attributes)
  • Sufficient colour contrast between text and background colours
  • The website is navigable using a keyboard alone
  • The website is navigable using a screen reader
  • Interactive elements such as buttons and links have descriptive labels (aria-label attributes where appropriate)
  • Form fields are labelled and associated with their controls
  • The website does not use content that flashes more than three times per second
  • Text can be resized up to 200% without loss of content or functionality
  • The website does not use audio or video that plays automatically

3. Known Limitations

While we strive for full WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, we are aware of the following current limitations:

  • Property gallery lightbox functionality may present challenges for some keyboard users — we are working to improve keyboard navigation within the lightbox
  • Some property images sourced from third-party providers may have limited alternative text descriptions — we are in the process of reviewing these
  • PDF documents linked from this website (including legal documents in the documents section) may not be fully accessible — if you require an accessible format, please contact us

4. Technical Information

Watsons & Co is committed to making this website accessible, in accordance with the Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) Accessibility Regulations 2018 (where applicable) and our own accessibility standards.

This website is built using standard HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript. It is designed to be compatible with:

  • Current versions of Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microsoft Edge and Apple Safari
  • Screen readers including NVDA (Windows) and VoiceOver (macOS/iOS)
  • Windows High Contrast mode
  • Mobile devices and tablets running iOS and Android

5. Requesting an Alternative Format

If you need any information on our website in an alternative format — for example, accessible PDF, large print, easy read, audio recording or braille — please contact us:

  • Email: hello@watsonsandco.com
  • Telephone: +44 (0) 191 946 0123
  • Post: Watsons & Co, 14 Grey Street, Newcastle upon Tyne, NE1 6AE

We will consider your request and respond within 5 working days.

6. Reporting Accessibility Issues

We are always looking to improve the accessibility of our website. If you find any accessibility problems that are not listed in this statement, please contact us using the details above. We welcome your feedback and will aim to address any issues as quickly as possible.

If you are not happy with how we respond to your accessibility concerns, you can contact the Equality and Human Rights Commission (EHRC) via their Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS) at equalityadvisoryservice.com.

7. How We Test This Website

We test our website using a combination of:

  • Manual testing with keyboard navigation
  • Testing with VoiceOver on macOS and iOS
  • Automated testing using browser accessibility tools
  • Regular review against the WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines

The website was last reviewed for accessibility in May 2026.

8. Visiting Our Office

Our office at 14 Grey Street, Newcastle upon Tyne is located on the ground floor and is accessible to wheelchair users. If you have any specific accessibility requirements when visiting us, please let us know in advance so we can make appropriate arrangements.
