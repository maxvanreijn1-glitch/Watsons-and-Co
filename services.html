Whether you are selling a cherished family home or seeking your next exceptional property, our sales team brings unrivalled knowledge of the Newcastle and Northumberland prime market to every instruction.

  • Qualified buyer vetting
  • Dedicated sale progression

For Landlords

Lettings &
Management

Our lettings service is built around the needs of landlords who want the very best tenant and the very least hassle. We manage a carefully selected portfolio of prime residential properties across Newcastle and the surrounding area.

From a fully managed service — where we handle everything from maintenance to rent collection — to a straightforward tenant-find service, we tailor our approach to exactly what you need.

  • Expert rental valuation
  • Rigorous tenant referencing
  • Tenancy agreement preparation
  • Rent collection and arrears management
  • Full property management service

03

Know Your Value

Property
Valuation

Our valuations are built on rigorous analysis of the prime North East market — not algorithms or automated estimates. We visit every property and deliver a precise, honest assessment of its value and its potential.

Whether you are planning to sell, remortgage, or simply wish to understand what your property is worth in today's market, our team will provide a thorough, market-led appraisal at no cost and with no obligation.

  • Free, no-obligation home visit
  • Comparable sales analysis
  • Current market conditions report
  • Honest pricing guidance
  • Advice on presentation and timing

04

For Investors

Investment
Advisory

Newcastle and Northumberland continue to offer exceptional value for prime residential property investors. Strong rental demand, a growing professional population, and competitive yields make this one of the most compelling investment markets in the north of England.

Our investment advisory service provides bespoke guidance to clients looking to build or refine a residential property portfolio — from identifying the right acquisitions to maximising returns on existing holdings.

  • Market opportunity analysis
  • Yield and capital growth projections
  • Off-market acquisition sourcing
  • Portfolio review and strategy
  • Introductions to specialist advisors
