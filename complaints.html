Complaints Procedure

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Version 1.0

Our Commitment Watsons & Co is committed to providing the highest standard of service. If you feel we have fallen short of this commitment, we want to hear from you. We take all complaints seriously and will deal with them promptly, fairly and professionally.

1. How to Make a Complaint

If you are dissatisfied with any aspect of our service, you should raise your concern in the first instance with the member of staff dealing with your matter. In many cases, concerns can be resolved quickly at this stage.

If you remain dissatisfied, you may submit a formal complaint to us using any of the following methods:

  • In writing: Watsons & Co, 14 Grey Street, Newcastle upon Tyne, NE1 6AE (marked "Formal Complaint")
  • By email: hello@watsonsandco.com (subject line: "Formal Complaint")
  • By telephone: +44 (0) 191 946 0123 (ask to speak with a Director)

Please provide your name, contact details, the address of the property concerned, a clear description of your complaint, and copies of any relevant correspondence or documents.

2. Our Internal Complaints Process

We will handle your formal complaint in the following stages:

  1. Acknowledgement: We will acknowledge your complaint in writing within 3 working days of receipt.
  2. Investigation: We will investigate your complaint fully. This may involve reviewing correspondence, speaking with the staff involved, and considering all relevant information.
  3. Written Response: We will provide a full written response within 15 working days of receiving your complaint. If we require more time to complete our investigation, we will contact you to explain why and provide a revised timescale. In any event, we will provide a final written response within 8 weeks.

Our written response will set out our findings, explain what has happened, and where appropriate propose a resolution. If your complaint is upheld, we will apologise and confirm what steps we will take to put things right.

3. If You Remain Dissatisfied — The Property Ombudsman

If you are not satisfied with our final written response, or if 8 weeks have passed since you first submitted your complaint without resolution, you have the right to refer your complaint to The Property Ombudsman (TPO).

The Property Ombudsman Milford House, 43–55 Milford Street, Salisbury, Wiltshire SP1 2BP
Tel: 01722 333306
Email: admin@tpos.co.uk
Website: www.tpos.co.uk

Important: You must refer your complaint to the TPO within 12 months of our final written response.

The TPO's service is free of charge and independent. The TPO will review your complaint impartially and may require us to pay compensation if they find in your favour. We are bound by the TPO's decisions.

Our TPO membership number is O-123456. A copy of the TPO Consumer Guide is available from the TPO or from us on request.

4. Additional Redress Options

In addition to the TPO, you may also have access to the following:

  • Consumer protection: Trading Standards via the Citizens Advice consumer service (0808 223 1133)
  • HMRC (for AML concerns): HMRC's anti-money laundering supervision complaints process
  • ICO (for data protection concerns): ico.org.uk or 0303 123 1113

5. Confidentiality

All complaints and related correspondence are treated with strict confidentiality. Information relating to your complaint will only be shared with those directly involved in investigating it and with any relevant regulatory bodies as required.

6. Learning from Complaints

We keep a record of all complaints received and review them regularly to identify patterns and opportunities to improve our services. We are committed to learning from any complaints we receive.
