Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How quickly can you arrange a property valuation?

In most cases we can arrange a valuation visit within 24 to 48 hours of your enquiry. Our valuations are carried out by a Director or Senior Negotiator with direct experience of your local market, not an automated estimate. There is no charge and no obligation.

Do you handle off-market property sales?

Yes. A considerable number of the properties we sell never appear on the open market. If you are considering selling and value discretion, or if you are a buyer seeking access to properties before they are publicly listed, please contact us directly. We maintain an active list of registered buyers and introduce buyers and sellers privately wherever possible.

What areas do you cover?

We focus on the prime residential market across Newcastle upon Tyne and Northumberland. Our core areas include Jesmond, Gosforth, Ponteland, Sandyford, Heaton, the Quayside, and the wider city. In Northumberland we cover the full county, with particular expertise in the villages and market towns of the Tyne Valley, the coastal strip from Alnmouth to Bamburgh, and the rural hinterland.

What are your sales fees?

Our standard sole agency fee is 1.5% of the final sale price (inclusive of VAT), subject to a minimum fee of £3,000 inclusive of VAT. All fees are agreed and confirmed in writing before instruction. There is no fee payable if we do not sell your property. For a full breakdown of our charges, please see our Fees & Charges Schedule.

Are you members of a redress scheme?

Yes. Watsons & Co is a member of The Property Ombudsman (TPO) redress scheme, membership number O-123456. We are also registered with HMRC for anti-money laundering supervision, and we hold Client Money Protection insurance through Propertymark. Full regulatory details appear in the footer of every page of this website.

Can I view a property at the weekend?

Absolutely. Our office is open Monday to Friday 9:00am – 6:00pm and Saturday 10:00am – 4:00pm. We also regularly arrange viewings outside of these hours by appointment. Please contact us to discuss what works best for you and we will do our very best to accommodate.
