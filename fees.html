Transparency Notice In accordance with The Estate Agents Act 1979, the Consumer Protection from Unfair Trading Regulations 2008, and guidance from The Property Ombudsman, we are required to provide clear and transparent information about our fees before you enter into any service agreement with us. All fees below are inclusive of VAT where stated.

Our sales fees are charged on a "no sale, no fee" basis unless otherwise agreed in writing.

2. Lettings — Landlord Fees

We offer three levels of lettings service. All fees are subject to VAT at 20%.

Service LevelFeeWhat's Included
Tenant Find Only 75% of first month's rent (inc. VAT), minimum £900 Marketing, accompanied viewings, tenant referencing, tenancy agreement preparation, move-in check-in report, deposit registration.
Rent Collection 10% of monthly rent (inc. VAT) All Tenant Find services plus monthly rent collection and arrears chasing, rent statement provision.
Fully Managed 14% of monthly rent (inc. VAT) All Rent Collection services plus full property management, maintenance co-ordination, periodic inspections, checkout report, deposit negotiation and return.

4. Investment Advisory

Fees for our investment advisory service are agreed individually with each client prior to instruction, depending on the scope of the work required. A written fee proposal will be provided before any work commences. As a guide:

  • Initial consultation and market overview: Complimentary
  • Portfolio review and written strategy report: From £600 inc. VAT
  • Acquisition sourcing and transaction management: 1.5% of purchase price, minimum £3,000 inc. VAT

5. General Notes

  • All fees are subject to VAT at the prevailing rate (currently 20%) unless shown as inclusive of VAT
  • Our written service agreement, provided to you before instruction, will confirm the exact fees applicable to your instruction
  • Fees may be subject to negotiation at our discretion
  • This schedule is subject to change; please contact us to confirm current fees
